Balik Talent Big Data

Event Details:

Date: August 20 2024

Time: 8:30AM

Involment: Documentation Commitee


Reflection:

The Lecture was held on August 20 2024 at 8:30AM at the ATA Hall, I was part of the Documentation Committee and was organized by the Student Council.


The event was eye opening just like the Miller Web Dev lecture as It reassured my choice in enrolling in CCS, and similarly to Miller Web Dev the guest speaker was a former CCS Student and this time he talked about what it was like to work in a big data company that handles as you can guess large amounts of data, he talked about an experience where he was tasked with making a program that allowed the automatic turning on and off of the lights in a corporate building using sensors and such and that sounded very complicated and technical that I forgot most about it except for the part he said it was very difficult and demanding purely because of the amount of data that had to be processed because of the large amount of people that would set off the sensors.


The event really opening my eyes to what it would be like to work in a large data company and even though they probably pay you handsomely I would not want that as a job, and made me realize that the things we take for granted everyday such as online banking or even browsing our phones use so much data that I feel bad for the people who has to make these work flawlessly or there will be consequences and BIG consequences for anyone working with financials.


Because of the event I am more aware of the requirement of needing a large workforce to even open a website that handles even a tiny amount of traffic, it made me realize that so much of our modern infrastructure would fail if there was one error in a code somewhere

SUCC Success [6th Week]

Event Details:

Date: October 13 2024

Time: 1:00PM

Involment: Participant


Reflection:

The Success was held on October 13 2024 at 1:00PM at the COPVA screen room and was organized by SUCC.


This SUCCESS was near the end of my time as an SUCC Macro and what an experience it was, If you didn’t know the SUCCESS is what the elder members of SUCC [Silliman University Camera Club] would make us Macros [aspiring members/newbies] do every week. Every week we were given a prompt like “long exposure” or any kind of type of photography and we were given until the next SUCCESS to take photos and submit it and during the SUCCESS there would a guest speaker, usually a former member of the SUCC or a well-known photographer in Dumaguete City we were even given a lecture by a photographer who documented wars before social media was a thing [I know I should know his name but it was earlier in my time as a Macro] but after the lecture which is mostly on photography skills we would have to present our photos in front of the elders and explain our methods, our ideas and so fourth and the best photos of the week would be uploaded to the SUCC page and would increase you chances of becoming a full-time member of SUCC [you can check out my submissions through my Facebook ‘Kurt Jumawan’].


My time as an SUCC Macro has really changed me a lot I have always loved taking photos but its usually with my phone camera and whenever I do get my hands on a camera it is always In auto, but my time with SUCC taught me how to use my camera and develop my skills for my hobby that is photography, and even though I was not selected to become a full time member of SUCC I still loved and will cherish my experience with them because it allowed me to make new friends and made me love photography even more.


Because of this SUCCESS and my time as an SUCC Macro I see the world with the thought “how would I capture this moment”, I learned so many valuable lessons and skills during my time and it really would allow me to pursue my hobby of photography to the next level.
