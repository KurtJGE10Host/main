CCS Opening Devotion

Event Details:

Date: August 19 2024

Time: 8:00AM

Involment: Documentation Commitee


Reflection:

This opening devotion was held inside the Silliman church at 8:00AM during the start of the school year, I was part of the documentation committee taking pictures of the mass.


This was not my first opening devotion because it is not my first year in Silliman, I am originally a shiftee from the College of Engineering after failing this summer term putting me behind by a year I decided to get a fresh start and shifted to the college of computer studies as a IT student, during the mass I learned several new skills about photography because of the fact I was part of the documentation committee.


I’m not really a religious person as I barely go to church and attend masses but this put me at ease but this marked my first event as a CCS student and also marks the start of a new chapter in my life after shifting from CED.


Because of this convocation I can now call myself a CCS student as this solidified my place In CCS with a blessing.

Sunday Mass

Event Details:

Date: October 13 2024

Time: 10:00AM

Involment: Spectator [1]


Reflection:

The Sunday mass was held at the cathedral 10:00AM on October 13 2024.


I am not really a religious person, but every Sunday I go to church, not to attend mass but to light a candle and pray, I personally believe that is a better way of showing your faith in the lord attending mass but this Sunday I decided to attend mass not only because it would be an entry into the journal but also because I haven’t attended mass ever since I went to college, whenever I go mass I usually go with my family but since I am far away from them this is the first time I went to mass on my own.


Going to mass alone made me feel sad but also glad that I am now independent and I am not forced to go to mass every Sunday, but I do feel a little bit of melancholy because even though Sundays in my family is reserved for masses we usually go eat at a fancy restaurant afterwards for family time, It made me realize that I miss my family much more than I thought.


Because of this mass I am more aware of how important it is even as a bonding event for families.

Event Details:

Date: October 14 2024

Time: 9:00AM

Involment: Spectator


Reflection:

This Convocation was on October 14 2024 at 9:00AM and was held in the Luce auditorium


This Convocation was my first one that’s because during my freshmen year as a CED student I may or may not have forgot about it and missed the event so this was my first time actually attending a convocation during the Founders Week in Silliman, and as always I am stunned by Luce auditorium I’ve never been in a theater like it, we barely had anything like this in my home city and reminds me that I am very lucky to be studying at Silliman University.

CCS Midweek Convocation

Event Details:

Date: November 20 2024

Time: 6:00pm

Involment: Spectator


Reflection:

This Midweek Convocation was held at the Silliman Church 6:00PM on November 20 2024.


This convocation was held at night and in the rain and still a lot of us turned up wet and damp, because of this event was held nearing the end of November it was probably gonna be the last church event I will attend for the semester as it was nearing December and the end of the semester and I was missing only 1 even for my church so this was perfect, but this reminded me of how fast time goes by one minute we were just starting and the next we were already nearing the end of our semester, everyone in the church had differing experiences growing each one of us, speaking for myself I had a massive life affecting change in just this year, I changed my course completely changing the trajectory of my life.
